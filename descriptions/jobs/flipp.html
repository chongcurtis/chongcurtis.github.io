Flipp was an absolute dream-come-true for 17-year-old me. It was the perfect match because they are the largest company I worked at and I was the youngest intern they took in.

As an intern, I built a resume classifier to predict if a resume would receive an interview. To build this classifier I built an entire ML pipeline. Firstly, I built a top-notch resume parser that beat Lever’s parsing by miles; this cleaned data for normalization. After running my resumes through traditional NLP techniques (stemming, n-grams, lemmatization, etc.), I created many bags of words and TF-IDF embeddings for my final model, an SVM ensemble.

Presenting my data explorations with weekly graphs was a highlight of my internship. I also loved staying late to train another model and try their HTC Vive for the first time.
