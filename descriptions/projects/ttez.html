At the climathon, our team wanted to describe the city's GTFS dataset and identify poor performing routes to recommend route changes. We began by calculating how much co2 one would emmit on an average bus ride. After we mined the numbers, we placed this information on an ios app so riders can identify which routes are the most polluting. Although the city switches to smaller buses during slow hours, we discovered that many buses still travel with less than 10% capacity at most times. Overall, our analysis won the team people's choice awards and identified areas for improvement in regards to carbon emmisions on the TTC.
