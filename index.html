MaxQ


As Canada's first space accelerator, MaxQ aims to lead space startups to success by providing mentorship and capital to emerging start-ups. At my time as an intern at the accelerator, I assisted one of their companies Skywatch by creating an API that parses HDF files (from NASA's satellites) into JSON. This data is then inserted into a Postgres database where third parties can query the API by geological region and time at a future date.

AgriGate

http://res.cloudinary.com/dj2eq8czc/image/upload/v1475448957/agrigate_izu0cv.jpg

Even in Canada, there is a huge inefficiency in the production of food. This problem is worsening along with the exponential growth of the human population, and very little seems to be being done about it. We were inspired to improve how farmers collect insights on the current state of their farm so we used Nasa's L4 satellite data and Ontario's land surveys to deliver a free and easy-to-use open-source tool. This web app can project crop prices until the year 2020 and can also deliver heat and moisture data in any region of the world. With the data gathered, farmers can predict when it is best to aggregate, harvest, and plant crops.

FinTech

http://res.cloudinary.com/dj2eq8czc/image/upload/v1468226326/fintechLargest_ycddax.jpg

Fintech is a mobile app centered around the ease of access for exterminators and environmentalist to track the spawning of the Asian Carp. In the 1970s, the predatorial Asian Carp found its way into the great lakes, and today they pose a threat to more than a hundred aquatic species that inhabit the waters. Fintech proposes a preventative solution by providing researchers and exterminators geological and environmental data of possible Asian Carp spawning locations. Through the use of water flow and temperature data, Fintech is able to predict where the Asian carp may spawn and delivers that data in a quick and elegant solution.

MinoTour

http://res.cloudinary.com/dj2eq8czc/image/upload/v1468228792/minotourMain_elcpfq.jpg

Have you ever been on vacation? Wondering around in your dream city, visiting Eiffel Tower, or the Louvre? Many of us would surely remember the emotions attributed to those historic sites. Although our vacations are centered around attractions, they are really about creating memories that last. So that is why we built MinoTour, an app that uses your contextual information to recommend the best tourist attractions. It uses your current contextual information through the Flybits API to tailor the best attractions that would impact you the most. For example, if you're in China and you've walked 5km, then you may feel fatigued. As a result, our algorithms would conclude that you are tired and will start suggesting nearby attractions to shorten travel distance. It will also promote indoor attractions to stray you away from unpleasant clouds. These variables are just some of many that MinoTour uses to help provide users memories that will last a lifetime.

Co Worm

http://res.cloudinary.com/dj2eq8czc/image/upload/v1469999411/co-worm_kqhhwy.png

Even with access to social media, engaging an audience from a stage is still an issue. Although the proficiency of the speaker is crucial to audience engagement, nothing compares to games carried out with the audience. With the same concept of "Twitch Plays Pokemon", Co Worm is a communal game that encourages crowds of people to achieve a single goal through collaboration. With some modification of the Html Joystick and Sinous, players could move a snake around on a screen by sending movement commands to a central Node server. The goal of the game is to survive as long as possible by avoiding meteors that fly across the screen. In addition, these scores are saved in a MongoDB database so different crowds can compete for the throne. All in all, Co Worm is a great way to bring the community closer together and I hope speakers abroad will find it useful when engaging their crowds!

SentiMint

http://res.cloudinary.com/dj2eq8czc/image/upload/v1473814778/sentimint_1_sogk9l.jpg

In many offices, managers do not understand their employee's health and wellbeing. In most cases, workers could be healthier and become more productive if they were placed in a separate team within the organization. To combat this issue, my team and I built SentiMint to help managers position employees in their areas of strength. The app uses a Slack bot to analyze group sentiment and displays their results it onto a web UI. Using the Indico API, the app also retrieves the Myers-Briggs personality types for every individual. With this information, along with live data collected from their interview and cover letter, the app makes a conclusive executive judgment that assigns the user to an appropriate slack channel that suits their personality.

Wattpad Statistics

http://res.cloudinary.com/dj2eq8czc/image/upload/v1473918557/wattpadMap_aqhjfh.png

Every month, 40 million users sign into Wattpad to engage with the princes and heroes within each other's lives. But what most can't comprehend, is the amount of user data Wattpad collects with every login. In Toronto alone, a GB of user data is archived by the company. With all this data, my team and I decided to create a story recommendation system based on similar characteristics between authors and their readers. Using Apache Spark, we ran a k-means algorithm against each user's age, language, and gender to uncover similar features. In addition, we created a map visual that describes an author's and user's location on a world map. That way, we can inspect these correlations in a visual manner to deduce the reason for why an author has the following it has.
