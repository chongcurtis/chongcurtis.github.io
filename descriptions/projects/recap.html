No matter how swift our needle-like fingers are, none of us can incept the perfect study note. Too often we scramble to keep track of what was said and tend to forget the meaning behind the words we wrote. Recap envisions an editor that serves as a second pair of ears - quite literally. Whether you're in a conference or at a student lecture, our web app will record the speaker and pair up the recording with the words you type. Once you stop typing, we'll stop recording. This mechanism is synced directly with Firebase and delivers an autosave feature similar to one found in Google Docs. We also implemented the Wolfram Alpha API so you can search up concepts you don't understand right in the editor. Happy note taking!
