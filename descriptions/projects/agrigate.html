Even in Canada, there is a huge inefficiency in the production of food. This problem is worsening along with the exponential growth of the human population, and very little seems to be being done about it. As a team, we were inspired to improve how farmers collect insights on the current state of their farm so we used Nasa's L4 satellite data and Ontario's land surveys to deliver a free and easy-to-use open-source tool. This web app can project crop prices until the year 2020 and can also deliver heat and moisture data in any region of the world. With the data gathered, farmers can predict when it is best to aggregate, harvest, and plant crops.
