Have you ever been on vacation? Wondering around in your dream city, visiting the Eiffel Tower, or the Louvre? Many of us would surely remember the emotions attributed to those historic sites. Although our vacations are centered around attractions, they are really about creating memories that last. So that is why we built MinoTour, an app that uses your contextual information to recommend the best tourist attractions. It uses your current contextual information through the Flybits API to tailor the best attractions that would impact you the most. For example, if you're in China and you've walked 5km, then you may feel fatigued. As a result, our algorithms would conclude that you are tired and will start suggesting nearby attractions to shorten travel distance. It will also promote indoor attractions to stray you away from unpleasant clouds. These variables are just some of many that MinoTour uses to help provide users memories that will last a lifetime.
