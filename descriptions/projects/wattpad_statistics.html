Every month, 40 million users sign into Wattpad to fall in love with their literary heroes. Although the readers come for the stories, it is Wattpad's metrics that bounds their return. In Toronto alone, a GB of user data is archived by the company, all describing a reader's journey through the app. With all this data, my team and I decided to create a story recommendation system based on similar characteristics between authors and their readers. Using Apache Spark, we ran a k-means algorithm against each user's age, language, and gender to uncover similar features. In addition, we created a map visual that describes an author's and user's location on a world map. That way, we can inspect these correlations in a visual manner to deduce the reason for why an author has the following it has.
