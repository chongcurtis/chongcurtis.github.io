I wrote this rigid box physics engine for my final project in gr 12 computer science. It comes packaged with support for kinematic motion, friction, elasticity, and even gravity! Inside the world editor, objects can be set to accelerate, have an initial velocity, and can be created at will.... say with a gun.

Well... if we're going to throw guns in there might as well make it a portal gun! Portals are teleporters that preserves an object's kinematic states to make the sandbox more frantic. I'm really proud of the overall solidity of the engine and if you have Greenfoot on your computer you can spin this up and play with fast-moving objects!
