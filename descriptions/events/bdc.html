The STEM Fellowship's Big Data Challenge is an annual competition that spurs youth to use data science and provide insights on issues. This year, the fellowship challenged 21 teams to use Altmetric data and identify opportunities for scientific research. Following their discoveries, contestants compiled an abstract that described their findings in detail. Impressed by our results, our team was invited to big data day and pitched our verdicts towards a panel of judges. After a tortuous journey, our team became a finalist, placed first, and published our abstract to the STEM Fellowship Journal.
