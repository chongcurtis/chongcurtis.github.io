In November, 33 schools from YRDSB sent several representatives to Hack2Action, a board-wide hackathon dedicated to the identification of greeenhouse gas inefficiencies. After passing the preliminary round, our team moved on to pitch to a panel of judges at Quest, an international conference which harbored 1000+ members of the education community. All in all, our team placed first at the conference and devised an incentive for the use of public transit.
